Hey there, internet friends!

Ilia and Pete are doing research on our media consumption habits. Neither of us care about your tinder profile pics, nor about any of your personal details — all we want to do is get a sense of the media pages y'all follow.

Privacy policy: This isn't going out to marketers (ugh!) or being monetized (money — the worst!). In fact, we don't even care about your name. All we want to do is find out which news organizations you follow on Facebook.
